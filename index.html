What We Do

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of residential and commercial plumbing services across Thika.

Leak Detection & Repair

Fast identification and repair of water leaks to prevent damage and conserve water. We handle pipe leaks, burst pipes, and hidden leaks.

Bathroom Installations

Full bathroom fitting including toilets, showers, sinks, bathtubs, and shower enclosures, with a neat and professional finish.

Pipe & Drainage Work

New pipe installations, pipe replacements, drain clearing, and sewer line maintenance for clean and efficient water flow.

Water Heater Services

Installation, servicing, and repair of electric and solar water heaters. We also advise on energy-efficient water heating solutions.

General Plumbing Repairs

Fixing dripping taps, faulty cisterns, overflowing tanks, and all day-to-day plumbing issues quickly and affordably.

New Construction Plumbing

Complete plumbing setup for new buildings, including rough-in plumbing, supply lines, and fixture installations.
